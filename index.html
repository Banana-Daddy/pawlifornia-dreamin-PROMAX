Issue 001 · The Philosophy

The question isn't
whether they should
be there —
it's how.

Pawlifornia Dreamin' exists because saying "I do" in front of the people you love — and the animals who love you back — shouldn't take a spreadsheet. We handle the logistics, the transport, the tiny tuxedos. You handle the vows.

Our team of handlers is insured, Pet CPR-trained, and Fear Free certified. We arrive before the first photo and leave after the last dance. Your four-legged family travels in a crate or seat belt (whichever they prefer), sips water from their own bowl, and walks down the aisle with someone who knows what to do when the treats run low at 4:14 PM.

We service the entire Coachella Valley, and we'll happily pack the van for a local desert destination — Joshua Tree, the high desert, LA-adjacent elopements, anywhere the golden hour is worth the drive.

"Pets aren't invited to weddings.
They're family being honored by them."

— Our working definition
